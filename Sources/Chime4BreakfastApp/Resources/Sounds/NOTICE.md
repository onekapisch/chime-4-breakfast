# Sound assets

All 14 `.wav` files in this folder are **original works**, synthesized from
scratch by the deterministic generator at [`scripts/gen-sounds.py`](../../../../scripts/gen-sounds.py).
They contain no samples, recordings, or third-party audio.

You may use, modify, and redistribute them for any purpose without attribution.

Regenerate them at any time with:

```bash
python3 scripts/gen-sounds.py
```

Output format: 16-bit mono PCM WAV at 44.1 kHz.
