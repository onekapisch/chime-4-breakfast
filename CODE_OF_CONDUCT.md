# Code of Conduct

Chime 4 Breakfast welcomes contributors who communicate with care, focus on the work, and respect the privacy of people sharing diagnostics or screenshots.

Harassment, personal attacks, discriminatory language, and posting another person's private data are not acceptable in issues, pull requests, or other project spaces.

Report conduct concerns privately to the repository owner. Reports will be handled promptly and discreetly. Contributors who violate these standards may have their participation restricted or removed.
